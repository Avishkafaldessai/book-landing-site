Key Framework: The 3-Minute Rule

Every habit that sticks starts with just three minutes. This isn't about limiting yourself to three minutes forever—it's about creating a starting point so small that it's impossible to fail.

  • Choose your desired habit
  • Scale it down to 3 minutes
  • Do it consistently for 2 weeks
  • Gradually increase duration
